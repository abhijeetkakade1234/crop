# Crop: Easily Split Your Images

## What is Crop?
Crop is a simple tool that helps you turn one big image (like a sheet of character drawings) into many small, separate images. 

It is perfect for anyone who has a big image and needs to save each part as a separate file without having to crop every single one by hand.

## 🚀 How to Use It

1. **Upload your image**: Just drag and drop your image onto the home page.
2. **Choose your grid**: Set how many rows and columns you want. You can see the changes happen in real-time.
3. **Remove Background**: If your image has a solid color background (like white or gray), you can use our "Background Removal" tool to make it transparent with one click.
4. **Save your work**: Click "Download all as ZIP" to get a folder with all your new small images.

## ✨ Cool Stuff
- **Smart Cleanup**: If you used AI to make your image and it has a "fake" checkerboard background, our Smart Mode can clean it up for you.
- **Preview**: See exactly what your final images will look like before you download them.
- **Fast**: No login required. Just upload, split, and save.

## 🛠 For Technical Users
Crop is built with React and uses your browser's power to process images locally. Your images are never uploaded to a server—everything happens right on your computer.
